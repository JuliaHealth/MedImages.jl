\section{The Mathematical Framework: Governing Equations}

The core of our approach is a 4-state UDE system integrated over a 300-hour therapeutic window.

\subsection{Activity Kinetics (Mechanistic PK)}
\begin{align}
    \frac{dA_{blood}}{dt} &= -(k_{10} + \lambda_{phys}) A_{blood} - \sum \text{Uptake} + \sum \text{Washout} \\
    \frac{dA_{free}}{dt} &= \text{Uptake} - (k_{out} + \lambda_{phys}) A_{free} \\
    \frac{dA_{bound}}{dt} &= k_3 A_{free} \left( 1 - \frac{A_{bound}}{B_{MAX}} \right) - (k_4 + \lambda_{phys}) A_{bound}
\end{align}

\subsection{Technical Insights into the Julia Advantage}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{LLVM-Fused Kernels}: Julia JIT-compiles the entire integration loop and the 3D convolutional derivative into a single, fused GPU kernel. This eliminates the overhead of jumping between the solver (C++/XLA) and the derivative function (Python/JAX-PRNG).
    \item \textbf{Memory Management}: Julia's non-allocating, in-place mutating kernels significantly reduce garbage collection pressure for the 786,433-element state vector compared to the vectorized map (vmap) approach used in JAX.
\end{itemize}
