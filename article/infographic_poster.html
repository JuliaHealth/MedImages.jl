The Volume Bottleneck

The Challenge

Preprocessing biobank-scale datasets (10,000+ studies) is an immense I/O and hardware bottleneck.

The Problem with Python

Reliance on Pickle/Pt dict-based serialization (MONAI, TorchIO) causes memory buildup and friction.

Zero-Serialization MedImages.jl: HDF5 Persistence + Fused Affine GPU Kernels.

Result: 7.2× Faster turnaround time, unlocking thousands of studies.

The Two-Language Barrier

The Challenge

Researchers prototype in high-level languages while relying on opaque C++ binaries (SimpleITK) for speed.

The Knowledge Gap

SimpleITK CPU filters act as a "brick wall" for native GPU acceleration and AD inspection.

LLVM JIT Unified Pure Julia Stack: Direct hardware acceleration via KernelAbstractions.jl.

Result: 135× Fused Affine & 115× Resampling Acceleration.

Differentiability & UDEs

The Challenge

Modeling voxel-level dosimetry requires integrating physical laws into ML training loops.

The SciML Solution

4-State Universal Differential Equations (UDE) natively integrate mechanistic knowns with neural residuals.

Expression Problem Solved Monte Carlo-level accuracy while avoiding framework "Walled Gardens".

Result: Pearson r = 0.9221 (Monte Carlo Fidelity).

Metadata Management

The Challenge

Decoupling spatial metadata during conversion to NumPy tensors leads to catastrophic "Metadata Drift".

The Innovation

BatchedMedImage explicitly binds physical metadata (origin, spacing, direction) to the voxel tensor.

Protected Metadata Clinical Metadata perfectly synchronized: SUV consistency < 1.5% deviation.

Result: Flawless quantitative alignment across massive multimodal batches.
